Origin of fried rice

Fried rice is thought to have originated in the city of Yangzhou in the eastern Jiangsu province of China, northwest of Shanghai, made from whole grain rice and many other ingredents and its preparation is different acrross different places and regions.it's not an indigenous Nigerian meal but has been incorporated into one of Nigerian's finest and mostly comusmed dishes.

Instructions(may or may not be correct)

  • Slice the following(Carrots,onions,bell peppers,liver)
  • Parboil the rice,sieve out water and set aside
  • Parboil the chicken and seasoning with curry,thyme,ginger,garlic,seasoned cubes,salt and onions
  • Put the pot on fire, pour in about 15cl of Vegetable oil.
  • Allow to heat then add the sliced onions,ginger and garlic stir for about 2 minutes
  • Add the sliced corrot and green beans, stir for another two minutes then add the curry powder and thyme
  • Add the gabbage,chicken stock,dry pepper and liver
  • Add the liver,sweet corn,bell peppers,seasoning cubes and stir
  • Add the parboiled rice and add little water
  • Cook for another 10 minutes
  • If the water has dried up and the rice is still hard,add a little water till the rice is soft
  • Pour some Vegetable oil in a fry pan and fry the chicken drumsticks (this is optional)

NOTES

  • I used jasmine rice in this recipe but you can use any long grain rice of yur choice.
  • Instead of frying the chicken you could broil it in the oven until golden brown
  • Your choice of vegetable is optional and peculiar to you
